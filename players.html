Mookie Betts

Position: RF
Bats: Right
Throws: Right
Ht: 5'9"
Team: Dodgers

Previously, Mookie Betts was on the Boston Red Sox, where he became the first player in MLB history to win the MVP, Silver Slugger, Gold Glove, batting title, and World Series in the same season. In 2020, he was traded to the Dodgers and signed the second-largest extension in MLB history, for 12 years, $365 million, and proceeded to win a second world championship.
